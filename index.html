FillLight is a professional camera app designed for selfie photography with built-in fill light functionality. The app provides customizable lighting effects to enhance your photos in any environment.

Key Features:

  • Fill Light Effects: 8 preset colors plus custom color picker
  • Camera Modes: Photo and delayed timer (3/5/10 seconds)

  • Multi-language: English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Arabic
  • Auto-save: Photos automatically saved to device gallery

üîßTroubleshooting

Camera Permission Issues

If the camera doesn't work:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Camera
  • Enable camera access for FillLight
  • Restart the app

Photos Not Saving

If photos aren't being saved:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Photos
  • Enable photo library access for FillLight
  • Check available storage space on your device

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest iOS version
  • Reinstall the app if issues persist

‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use FillLight with the rear camera?

A: Currently, FillLight is optimized for front-facing camera selfies. The fill light feature works best with the front camera.

Q: How do I change the app language?

A: The app follows your device's system language setting. Go to Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Language & Region to change your device language.

Q: Why does the app request microphone permission?

A: The microphone permission was used for video recording features. In the latest version, this feature has been simplified to focus on photo capture only.
